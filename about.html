About Me

I attend both California State, Monterey Bay and Hartnell College through the program CSin3. This rigorous program provides a year-round pathway for aspiring undergrad software engineers to graduate with their Bachelors Degree in 3 years. This program unites cohorts in a community based learning model that bridges the challenges faced as an undergraduate computer science student. With this model, students cultivate the necessary skills needed for the techfield and prepares them for big tech interviews.
